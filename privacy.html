Privacy Policy

What happens to my emails? Does someone read them?

No. Our servers process email, but no person on our team will ever read your messages.

Are my email addresses shared/sold?

No. We won't sell or share any data that is passed through our servers.

Are you going to send me spam?

No. We won't provide your information to any third parties, or try to sell you anything on behalf of a third party. We will send you periodic updates concerning our service, but we won't bug you constantly.
